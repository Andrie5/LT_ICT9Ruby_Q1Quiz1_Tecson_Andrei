How to Train Your Dragon


This was one of the best animated movies made for kids. The story, characters, and plot were well thought out and amazing despite the fact that it was a children's movie. I would always use to watch this movie on repeat when I was younger and it still has a special place for me today.

The Story

Hiccup is a young boy who is seen as a dissapointment among his village as he has never slain a dragon before. One night, he sets out to do just that, though things take some turns.
